Christmas at the Warner

December 24: 3:30 and 6:00 PM

Tickets

Get your FREE tickets today!

3:30PM 6:00PM

Tickets are free, but required for admission. The Warner will not allow anyone into the theater without a ticket. Children are welcome in the service and also need tickets. If your child is 2 or under and you want to take advantage of GraceKids childcare, those tickets are reserved separately.

We'll also be taking food and clothing donations to benefit the Erie City Mission, get more details here.

Give Back

We are honored to partner with The Erie City Mission to host a food and clothing drive on Christmas Eve. Any clothing and non-perishable food is welcomed. So clean out your cupboards and closets and bring donations with you to the Warner! This is a great way to give back this holiday season.

Childcare Reservation

3:30PM 6:00PM

Children’s programming will be provided for kids through age two, but only with a reservation. Due to limited space, the cost per child is $5. That $5 is a non-refundable donation that will go to the City Mission. Childcare check-in will happen on the north side of the Warner Theater at the First Niagara bank entrance off of 10th Street. There are a limited number of spots available, so please register as soon as possible.

Directions & Parking

Ramps are available on 8th Street between State and French streets and on 10th Street between State and French streets. The Erie Insurance Arena Ramp on 8th Street between French and Holland streets is also available. Handicap parking is available at the southeast corner of 8th and French streets.
